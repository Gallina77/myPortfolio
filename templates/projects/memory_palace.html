Memory Palace

Inspired from the Book "Mnemonic Memory Palace" from Sjur Midtun this shall be an introduction on how to train the technique of storing and memorizing any information you need to re-call. If you want to know more about this ancient technique, check the behind the scenes page of this mini project.

How to Build your Memory Palace

1.

As a Beginner build your Memory Palace from a location you know well. Close your eyes and imagine your Apartment, your house, the place where you work. Go through the room in one direction, e.g. clockwise and identify objects on which you can "hang" your information.

2.

Enter all objects, also called "pegs" and build your list.

3.

As a next step you need to build a list that you want to memorize. Use the speech button and

4.

As a final step click on "Build my Memory Palace". Close your eyes, let the images being created and your brain as it loves fun and crazy stuff. Now you have proof that you have a good memory.
